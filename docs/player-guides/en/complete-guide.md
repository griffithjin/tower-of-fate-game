## Getting Started

### 1. Register an Account
- Visit the Tower of Fate website
- Click "Sign Up" button
- Enter your email and password
- Verify your email address
- Complete your profile

### 2. Choose Your Theme
- Navigate to Settings → Theme
- Select from available themes:
  - Classic Tower
  - Modern Skyscraper
  - Mystical Castle
  - Space Station
  - Nature Harmony

### 3. Select a Card Deck
- Go to Card Collection
- Choose from 4 different decks
- Each deck has unique abilities and strategies
- Customize your deck with earned cards

### 4. Join a Game
- Click "Play Now" for quick match
- Or "Join Tournament" for competitive play
- Wait for matchmaking to complete
- Game starts when all players are ready

## Game Rules

### Basic Rules
- **4 Decks of Cards** (208 cards total)
- **13 Guards** with 13 cards each
- Match guard cards to climb the tower
- Avoid anger cards (punishment cards)
- First player to reach floor A wins

### Tower Structure
```
Floor A  - 🏆 Victory Floor
Floor K
Floor Q
Floor J
Floor 10
Floor 9
Floor 8
Floor 7
Floor 6
Floor 5
Floor 4
Floor 3
Floor 2
Floor 1  - Start
```

### Card Matching
- Play cards matching the current guard
- Each successful match advances one floor
- Wrong cards may trigger penalties
- Special cards have unique effects

### Anger Cards
- Red-marked cards are "Anger Cards"
- Drawing an anger card causes penalties:
  - Lose 1 turn
  - Move down 1 floor
  - Discard 2 cards
- Use strategy to avoid anger cards!

## Tips & Strategies

### Beginner Tips
1. **Learn the Card Types**
   - Regular cards (1-10)
   - Face cards (J, Q, K, A)
   - Special action cards
   - Anger cards (avoid!)

2. **Watch Your Opponents**
   - Observe their playing patterns
   - Predict their moves
   - Counter their strategies

3. **Manage Your Hand**
   - Don't play all good cards early
   - Save powerful cards for critical moments
   - Balance risk and reward

### Advanced Strategies

#### 1. Card Counting
- Keep track of played cards
- Calculate probability of remaining cards
- Make informed decisions

#### 2. Bluffing
- Pretend to have certain cards
- Force opponents to make mistakes
- Use psychological tactics

#### 3. Risk Management
- Calculate floor-by-floor risks
- Know when to play safe
- Know when to take risks

#### 4. Combo Chains
- Combine multiple card effects
- Create devastating combinations
- Maximize point gains

### Guard-Specific Tips

## Card System

### Card Rarity
- **Common** (Gray) - Basic cards
- **Uncommon** (Green) - Enhanced effects
- **Rare** (Blue) - Powerful abilities
- **Epic** (Purple) - Game-changing cards
- **Legendary** (Gold) - Ultimate power

### Special Cards

#### Shield Card
- Protects from anger effects
- Single use per game
- Cannot be stolen

#### Teleport Card
- Jump to any floor
- Skip intermediate floors
- Strategic positioning

#### Swap Card
- Exchange positions with opponent
- Great for comeback plays
- Use at critical moments

#### Double Card
- Next match counts as two
- Accelerate your climb
- Combo with other effects

## FAQ

### Q: How do I earn cards?
**A:** You can earn cards by:
- Winning games
- Completing daily quests
- Opening card packs
- Trading with other players
- Tournament rewards

### Q: What happens if I disconnect?
**A:** 
- Within 30 seconds: Auto-reconnect
- 30-60 seconds: AI takes over temporarily
- After 60 seconds: Game counts as forfeit

### Q: Can I play with friends?
**A:** Yes! Use the "Invite Friends" feature to:
- Create private rooms
- Set custom rules
- Play together regardless of rank

### Q: How does the ranking system work?
**A:** 
- Win games to earn ranking points
- Lose games to lose points
- Higher ranks = better rewards
- Seasonal rank resets

### Q: Are there in-game purchases?
**A:** 
- Cosmetic items only (themes, avatars)
- No pay-to-win mechanics
- All cards earnable through gameplay

### Q: How do tournaments work?
**A:** 
- Scheduled events with entry fees
- Bracket-style elimination
- Prize pool distribution
- Ranking points bonus
